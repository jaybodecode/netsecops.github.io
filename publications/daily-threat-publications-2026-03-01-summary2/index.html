Summary

This cybersecurity brief for March 1, 2026, covers a critical 10.0 CVSS zero-day vulnerability in Cisco SD-WAN software under active exploitation. The landscape is further defined by massive data breaches, with business services giant Conduent reporting a ransomware attack affecting over 25 million individuals, and Canadian Tire confirming a cloud misconfiguration exposed 38 million customer accounts. Other major incidents include a ransomware attack on the University of Hawaiʻi Cancer Center, the emergence of the 'Oblivion' Android RAT, and a surge in cyber hostilities in the Middle East linked to geopolitical tensions.
