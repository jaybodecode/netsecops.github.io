ADT Breached by ShinyHunters, China's Covert Router Networks Exposed, and New 'FIRESTARTER' Backdoor Hits US Gov

Publication Date: April 25, 2026

Summary

This edition covers a busy period in cybersecurity, highlighted by home security giant ADT confirming a major data breach by the ShinyHunters group via a vishing attack. An international advisory warns of China-linked APTs like Volt Typhoon building massive covert networks from compromised SOHO routers for espionage. CISA also revealed a sophisticated 'FIRESTARTER' backdoor on a federal agency's Cisco firewall that survives patches. Other major events include Microsoft patching an actively exploited SharePoint zero-day, multiple ransomware attacks on US entities, and the discovery of malicious crypto apps on Apple's App Store.
