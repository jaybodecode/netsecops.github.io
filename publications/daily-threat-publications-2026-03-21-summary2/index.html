Summary

A critical 24-hour period in cybersecurity saw a sophisticated supply chain attack compromise the popular Trivy scanner, injecting credential-stealing malware into CI/CD pipelines globally. Concurrently, medical tech giant Stryker suffered a devastating wiper attack from Iran-linked actors who abused Microsoft Intune to erase 80,000 devices. Other major incidents include the zero-day exploitation of a Cisco firewall flaw by the Interlock ransomware gang, a joint CISA/FBI warning about Russian intelligence phishing campaigns targeting messaging apps, and multiple large-scale data breaches at Aura and Telus Digital.
