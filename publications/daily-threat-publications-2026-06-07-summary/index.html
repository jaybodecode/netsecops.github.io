Publication Date: June 7, 2026

Summary

This edition covers the period of June 7, 2026, a day marked by severe supply chain and cloud security incidents. A sophisticated self-replicating worm named Miasma has compromised dozens of Microsoft and Red Hat packages on npm, leveraging a novel execution bypass. Concurrently, a massive data breach at Snowflake exposed data from 165 customers, including Ticketmaster, due to credential theft. Adding to the urgency, Cisco has disclosed an actively exploited, unpatched zero-day vulnerability in its Catalyst SD-WAN Manager. Other major developments include a surge in AI-driven phishing, a significant rise in mobile banking trojans, and a volatile ransomware market reacting to recent law enforcement takedowns.
