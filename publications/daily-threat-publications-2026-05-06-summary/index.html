Palo Alto Networks Zero-Day Under Active Attack; DAEMON Tools Hit by Major Supply Chain Compromise

Publication Date: May 6, 2026

Summary

A critical zero-day vulnerability (CVE-2026-0300) in Palo Alto Networks' PAN-OS is being actively exploited for remote code execution, forcing urgent mitigation as patches are pending. Concurrently, a sophisticated supply chain attack has trojanized official DAEMON Tools installers, deploying backdoors and advanced RATs against targeted government and scientific entities. This period also saw disclosures of new malware campaigns from Chinese and North Korean APTs, a critical RCE flaw in Apache HTTP Server, and a large-scale phishing attack detailed by Microsoft, highlighting a landscape dominated by high-impact vulnerabilities and state-sponsored threats.
