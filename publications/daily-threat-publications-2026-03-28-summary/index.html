Global Cyber Threats Escalate: China-Linked APTs, Critical Telegram Flaw, and Widespread Ransomware Attacks

Publication Date: March 28, 2026

Summary

This period saw a significant escalation in cyber threats, highlighted by the discovery of the 'Red Menshen' China-linked APT using stealthy BPFdoor implants to infiltrate global telecoms. A critical zero-click RCE vulnerability in Telegram put millions of users at risk. Ransomware attacks continued their onslaught, crippling the Jackson County Sheriff's Office in Indiana and Foster City, California, while the new 'Uragan' strain emerged with double-extortion tactics. Major Canadian telecoms and the European Commission suffered significant data breaches, and the EU moved forward with its Cyber Resilience Act, signaling a new era of security regulation for digital products.
