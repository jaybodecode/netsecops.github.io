Summary

A critical 24 hours in cybersecurity saw coordinated GPS spoofing attacks disrupt global transit, prompting a G7 legislative response. Meanwhile, new attack vectors emerged with "Bband Siphon" malware targeting Android hardware, "Latent Poisoning" creating hidden AI backdoors, and the "Cloud Sweep" group embedding ransomware in cold storage. Major data breaches at a telehealth platform and Crimestoppers exposed millions of sensitive records, while the Payload ransomware group targeted a Bahrain hospital, highlighting escalating threats across critical infrastructure, mobile devices, and data privacy.
