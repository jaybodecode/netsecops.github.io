Summary

This 24-hour period has been marked by severe, high-impact cybersecurity incidents, led by a catastrophic data breach of the Canvas LMS affecting millions of students and faculty worldwide during final exams. Adding to the pressure, a critical, unpatched 'Dirty Frag' zero-day vulnerability was disclosed for the Linux kernel, enabling immediate root access. Meanwhile, threat actors are actively exploiting zero-days in Palo Alto Networks firewalls and a Windows Shell flaw, while new ransomware (BARADAI) and cloud worm (PCPJack) campaigns have emerged. These events highlight escalating threats from supply chain attacks, sophisticated phishing, and the rapid weaponization of vulnerabilities.
